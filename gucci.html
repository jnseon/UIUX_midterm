GUCCI GOTHIC

Young farmer-punks appear in a surreal, mythological world, where an agricultural landscape and the changing weather emphasize the power of nature.

The Cruise 2019 campaign shot by Glen Luchford pictures a rural community where animals and people co-exist in harmony. Animals including a tiger, elephant, ostrich, sheep and llama depart for a wooden ark together with the characters from the campaign wearing the new collection by Alessandro Michele.
